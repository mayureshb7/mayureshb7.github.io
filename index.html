About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Clemson University International Center for Automotive Research with research focus area in autonomous vehicle/robotics planning and control.

I investigate learning-based planning and control methods that integrate large multimodal models (LMMs) with model-based reasoning and human-in-the-loop evaluation to improve the reliability of autonomous systems.
